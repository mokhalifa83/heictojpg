About HEICtoJPG

A fast, private, and free image conversion tool built for everyone.

Our Mission

HEIC and WebP are modern image formats that save space but aren't always compatible with older software, social media platforms, or photo printing services. Our mission is to provide a dead-simple tool that converts these files to the universally supported JPG format — instantly and without compromising your privacy.

Your files are processed on the fly and never stored. We don't upload your data to any cloud — it stays on the server only for the duration of the conversion.

Why Use This Tool?

Speed: Conversions happen in seconds. Just upload, click convert, and download.

Privacy: No accounts, no tracking, no saved files. What you convert stays between you and your browser.

Free: No paywalls, no credit cards, no hidden limits. Convert as many images as you need.

Quality: We use high-quality JPEG encoding at 90% quality to ensure your images look great.

Supported Formats

HEIC/HEIF: High Efficiency Image Format used by Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, Mac). HEIC saves up to 50% storage compared to JPG while maintaining quality.

WebP: Google's modern image format designed for the web with superior compression. WebP images are typically 25-35% smaller than JPG files at equivalent quality.

Our Commitment

HEICtoJPG was built with a simple philosophy: image conversion should be free, fast, and private. We don't ask for registration, we don't track your uploads, and we never share your files. Every conversion is processed securely and deleted immediately after download. No gimmicks, no upsells — just a tool that works.

Whether you're a photographer needing to deliver JPG files to clients, an iPhone user sending photos to friends on older devices, or a web developer working with WebP assets, HEICtoJPG makes the process seamless.
