Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's how we handle your data.

This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. Using HEICtoJPG means you trust us with your files — we don't take that lightly.

Last updated: January 2026

1. What Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to operate this service. When you upload an image, the file is temporarily stored on our server for processing. We do not collect personal information, log file contents, or track your usage across sessions.

2. How We Use Your Data

The image you upload is used solely for the purpose of converting it from HEIC or WebP to JPG format. We do not use your files for any other purpose.

3. File Retention

Uploaded files are deleted from our server immediately after conversion. The converted JPG file is available for download for a short period and is then automatically removed. We do not keep backups or copies of your images.

4. Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or any form of user tracking. If we display ads via a third-party provider (e.g., Google AdSense), they may use cookies in accordance with their own privacy policies. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not share your uploaded files with any third party. The conversion happens entirely on our server. If we integrate third-party advertising, those providers have their own privacy policies governing data collection.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect your data during transmission and processing. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
