How It Works

Three simple steps to convert your HEIC or WebP image to JPG.

No technical skills needed. The entire process takes just a few seconds — upload, convert, and download your high-quality JPG file.

📤

Upload Your Image

Drag and drop a HEIC or WebP file onto the upload zone, or click to browse your computer. Files up to 50 MB are supported. We accept .heic, .heif, and .webp file types.

2
⚙️

Convert to JPG

Click "Convert to JPG" and our server instantly processes your image. We use high-quality JPEG encoding at 90% quality so your image looks its best.

3
⬇️

Download

Once converted, click the download button to save your new JPG file. The original file is deleted from our server immediately after conversion.
